Translating Signs, Transforming Communication

At FSL Translator, we are dedicated to shattering communication barriers that have long separated deaf and hearing individuals. Our revolutionary web application is a testament to our commitment to fostering understanding, connection, and inclusivity.

About Us

Our app, the Filipino Sign Language Translator, represents the result of extensive research, dedicated development efforts, and a profound commitment to eliminating communication obstacles faced by the Deaf and Hard of Hearing community in the Philippines. Crafted with a focus on user-friendliness, precision, and accessibility, our goal is to enable individuals to communicate proficiently in Filipino Sign Language (FSL) with the wider society.

  • Watch as your signs are recognized by our app, offering a text translations.
  • Users can use their device's camera for sign language recognition, making communication more intuitive.
  • Our app provides a powerful visual communication tool, enabling deaf and mute to express themselves and understand others effectively.
